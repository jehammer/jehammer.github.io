About Me

Hi there! My name is Joshua Hammer. When not working you can often find me working on a variety of passion projects, from a discord bot for my ESO Guild, to worldbuilding for fun TTRPG's with my friends. Creativity is highly important to me, it helps when coming up for solution to tasks given on the job and beyond.

First taking apart old Windows 95 machines and poking around inside to see what everything did, to my first programming courses in High School and eventually to my A.S. in Software Development and B.S. in Computer Science. I have experience with Java, C++, C#, Python, HTML5, CSS3, Javascript, and some XML experience.

I have certificates in Java and C++ development, and Scrum Foundations. I have also recieved training in Spring Boot, JUnit, Mockito, and OpenAPI. Currently, I am employed as a Java Cloud Solutions Developer.
